The Midnight Library

by Matt Haig

Fiction

Fantasy

Synopsis:

The Midnight Library is a thought-provoking novel that explores the life of Nora Seed, a woman filled with regret and unhappiness. After a series of personal failures, Nora finds herself in a magical library between life and death. Each book in the library represents a different life Nora could have lived if she had made different choices. Through exploring these alternate lives, Nora discovers the profound truth about regret, possibility, and what it means to live a meaningful life.
